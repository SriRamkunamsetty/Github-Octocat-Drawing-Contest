# Stained-Glass Algorithm Octocat

## Description
A complex stained-glass window where each part is hand-cut translucent glass joined by dark lead cames.

## Why I chose the design
To turn abstract mathematical functions into a physical, classical art form.

## Uniqueness
Casts intricate fractal or Voronoi patterns onto the background through light and texture.

## Artistic philosophy
Renaissance cathedral architecture meets abstract geometry. It represents cutting-edge science through an ancient medium.

## Technology/design approach used
Translucent patterned glass rendering with heavy black lead outlines and cinematic lighting.

## Brand identity feel
Majestic, striking, and timeless.
