# Latent Space Explorer Octocat

## Description
The Octocat navigating a landscape of massive, translucent hyper-cubes and 3D data clusters in a deep-sea environment.

## Why I chose the design
To translate mathematical concepts into a tangible, explore-able environment.

## Uniqueness
Replaces traditional coral with hyper-cubes, turning abstract data into a physical landscape.

## Artistic philosophy
Mathematical exploration. It visualizes the vastness of information storage as a navigable space.

## Technology/design approach used
Low-poly abstract geometry with a soft glassmorphism effect on the central figure.

## Brand identity feel
Exploratory, geometric, and serene.
