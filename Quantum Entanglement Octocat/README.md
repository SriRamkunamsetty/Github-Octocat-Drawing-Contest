# Quantum Entanglement Octocat

## Description
An entity made of shifting light and entangled probability waves, with duplicate ghost-like versions of itself.

## Why I chose the design
To explore the science-meets-art concept of quantum states and non-linear existence.

## Uniqueness
The structure is formed by glowing cyan, magenta, and gold particles rather than solid lines.

## Artistic philosophy
Circuit-punk meets cosmic. It represents the deep science of complex computational systems through abstract light.

## Technology/design approach used
Particle-based light rendering with cinematic lighting and a dark background.

## Brand identity feel
Intellectual, abstract, and mysterious.
